About Me

Welcome to MatchaTime, your go-to destination for all things matcha! Our matcha shop is located in the heart of the city and offers a wide range of matcha-based drinks and snacks for you to enjoy.
At MatchaTime, we take great pride in serving only the highest quality matcha sourced from Japan. Our drinks are made fresh to order and can be customized to suit your preferences. Whether you prefer a classic matcha latte, a refreshing matcha smoothie, or a decadent matcha frappe, we've got you covered. At MatchaTime, we believe that health and wellness are key to a happy and fulfilling life. That's why we offer educational materials and workshops on the health benefits of matcha and other healthy lifestyle choices. Our goal is to empower our customers to make informed choices and live their best lives. So come on in and experience the calming and energizing benefits of matcha at MatchaTime. We look forward to serving you!
